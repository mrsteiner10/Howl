QMesh – Quantum Networking System

QMesh is a decentralized quantum networking platform that leverages diamond NV-center memory, h-BN substrates, and photonic entanglement for next-gen quantum communication.

Its core components include:

  • Self-evolving quantum protocol using hybrid QML + Reinforcement Learning
  • Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) and quantum blockchain (QMesh Token)
  • Casimir-effect–powered autonomous quantum nodes (QMesh Zero)
  • FSO laser uplinks for satellite-to-ground mesh (QMesh Cosmic)

QMesh aims to revolutionize secure communication in IoT, aerospace, defense, and blockchain ecosystems.
