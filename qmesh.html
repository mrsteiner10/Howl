QMesh™ – Decentralized Quantum Networking

QMesh is a quantum networking paradigm based on adaptive quantum nodes utilizing NV-center memory, h-BN substrates, and hybrid AI protocols (QSVM + Reinforcement Learning). Designed for future-proof communication in IoT, blockchain, and space applications, QMesh integrates quantum key distribution (QKD), self-powered nodes (Casimir effect), and a quantum-secure blockchain ecosystem using QMesh Tokens (QMT). The system is scalable, secure, and highly autonomous.
