HoloCure – Decentralized BioQuantum Platform

HoloCure proposes a radical therapeutic platform combining RNA logic switches, nanoshell targeting, and quantum biology. It aims to cure major cancers (breast, lung, colorectal, prostate) using bioquantum nodes (HoloNodes), a predictive HoloBrain, and the HoloShell delivery system. The platform adapts using multi-omics and self-repairing bio-logic circuits, allowing real-time updates in treatment strategy and adaptive immune integration. It represents a new frontier in integrative precision therapy and bioholography.
