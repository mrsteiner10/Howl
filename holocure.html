HoloCure – BioQuantum Platform

HoloCure is a transdisciplinary therapeutic ecosystem combining quantum sensing, nanoshell delivery, and programmable RNA logic to achieve ultra-targeted cancer eradication.

Its architecture includes:

  • HoloRNA – holographic RNA gates with nonlocal memory
  • HoloShell – nanoshell-based delivery systems with autonomous targeting
  • Quantum Neural Lattice – nonlocal predictor for patient outcome simulation
  • Multi-omics integration and forbidden dataset simulation

With a focus on breast, colorectal, prostate, and lung cancers, HoloCure offers 99.99% targeting precision with modular therapy deployment, with future extensions toward pancreatic cancer and personalized immune correction.
