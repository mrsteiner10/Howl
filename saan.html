SAAN: Self-Aware Autonomous Nodes

SAAN introduces a decentralized architecture where nodes are capable of real-time self-awareness and adaptive learning. Each node employs Q-Learning and TinyML for minimal-overhead intelligence, predicting failures and adapting behavior without a central controller. The system is optimized for smart cities, defense infrastructure, and deep space networks. The core includes a lightweight awareness kernel and a predictive behavior layer, enabling autonomous evolution of the network over time.
