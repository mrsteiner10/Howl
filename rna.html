RNA Toggle Switch

The RNA Toggle Switch is a synthetic biological circuit that functions as a multi-input logic gate, using RNA aptamers and responsive nanoshells to control gene expression with high precision.

This system supports dynamic regulation of cellular states by integrating multiple RNA inputs into a toggle-based architecture, enabling smart control of therapeutic payloads, metabolic pathways, and synthetic responses inside living cells.

Features include:

  • Multi-layered RNA sensing and filtering
  • Environmentally triggered nanoshell activation
  • Modular genetic logic compatible with CRISPR/Cas and ribozyme platforms
  • Microfluidic integration for lab-on-chip diagnostics and feedback therapy

Potential applications span cancer therapy, biosensing, programmable immunity, and synthetic cellular computation.
