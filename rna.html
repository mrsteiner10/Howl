Multi-Input RNA Toggle Switch

This invention presents a next-generation RNA-based synthetic biology logic system designed to act as a programmable toggle switch within living cells. It supports multiple environmental inputs, allowing for conditional cellular behavior control. Integrated nanoshell filtering, aptamer-based precision targeting, and microfluidic compatibility make it suitable for personalized medicine, smart therapeutics, and cellular robotics. Applications include cancer therapy, adaptive biosensors, and gene circuit design.
